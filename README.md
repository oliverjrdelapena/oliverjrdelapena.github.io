# Oliver Jr Dela Peña 
Oliver Jr Dela Peña
Filipino TikTok Content Creator and Digital Personality based in the Philippines. He is known for his viral TikTok dance performances and engaging short-form entertainment content.
Oliver has accumulated over 5.9 million total views across his platforms, including a standout TikTok dance video that reached 2.1 million views. He currently has 63.9K+ followers on TikTok, continuing to grow his online presence through creative and trend-driven content.
Born on June 3, 2007, in Cebu City, Philippines, and raised in Bacolod City, Oliver represents the new generation of Filipino digital creators making an impact on social media.
